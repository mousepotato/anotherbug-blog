Octopus Garden

Privacy Policy — Last updated March 30, 2026

Core Principle

Your Data Stays Yours

Octopus Garden stores your journal entries in your own private GitHub repository or locally on your device. We operate zero servers that hold your personal data.

GitHub Authentication

Sign-in uses OAuth via a Cloudflare Worker auth proxy. No data is logged during this process. The auth proxy exists solely to keep the OAuth client secret off your device.

GitHub App Permissions

The app requests only two permissions: Contents (read/write, for your journal repo) and Metadata (read-only, required by GitHub). Nothing else.

Location Data

Location access is optional. If granted, your coordinates are sent only to the Open-Meteo API to fetch local weather. They are never stored or tracked by us.

Media Library

Media library access is write-only and used exclusively to save AI-generated images you choose to keep. We never read or index your photo library.

Zero tracking

Analytics & Advertising

There are no analytics SDKs, no crash reporters, no ad networks, and no third-party tracking of any kind in Octopus Garden.

Third-Party Services

GitHub API — stores your journal data per GitHub's own privacy policy.

Open-Meteo — receives coordinates for weather lookups only; no tracking.

Cloudflare Workers — handles the OAuth flow; no personal data is retained.

Data Retention

We retain zero data server-side. Deleting the GitHub App authorization or your journal repository permanently removes all data. You are in full control.

Children's Privacy

Octopus Garden is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
